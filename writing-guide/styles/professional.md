# Style: Professional

## Best Fit
Status updates, strategy docs, B2B articles, leadership communication.

## Rules

### Rule: Be Direct and Specific
Description: Prioritize clarity and decision-useful detail.
Negative example: "We are leveraging multiple opportunities for improvement."
Positive example: "We will reduce onboarding steps from 9 to 6 this quarter."

### Rule: Keep Tone Neutral and Confident
Description: Avoid hype and emotional overstatement.
Negative example: "This incredible update will absolutely transform everything."
Positive example: "This update improves handoff speed and reduces rework risk."

### Rule: Close With Ownership
Description: End sections with owner, timeline, or action.
Negative example: "We should probably revisit this sometime."
Positive example: "Product Ops will ship the revised template by Friday."
