# Style: Minimalist

## Best Fit
High-signal summaries, executive updates, short educational posts.

## Rules

### Rule: Cut to Essential Meaning
Description: Keep only what changes reader understanding.
Negative example: Redundant restatements and decorative modifiers.
Positive example: One claim, one proof, one implication.

### Rule: Keep Sentences Clean and Lean
Description: Use simple syntax and direct verbs.
Negative example: "There is a tendency for teams to be in a position where..."
Positive example: "Teams stall when decisions are not explicit."

### Rule: Preserve Precision While Being Brief
Description: Short writing must still be accurate.
Negative example: Over-compressed statement that drops key constraints.
Positive example: "This works for weekly updates, not emergency incident comms."
