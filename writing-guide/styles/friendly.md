# Style: Friendly

## Best Fit
Creator updates, educational newsletters, onboarding content, community posts.

## Rules

### Rule: Sound Human, Not Casual to the Point of Vague
Description: Warm tone should still preserve precision.
Negative example: "Hey friends, just vibing with some thoughts today."
Positive example: "Quick win: use one concrete example in your intro to make your point easier to trust."

### Rule: Use Simple Sentences With Inviting Language
Description: Keep syntax easy without flattening meaning.
Negative example: "Pursuant to the foregoing, the procedural sequence is..."
Positive example: "Here is the sequence that makes this easier."

### Rule: Keep Encouragement Actionable
Description: Support readers with practical next steps.
Negative example: "You got this."
Positive example: "Pick one paragraph and rewrite it with a clearer first sentence."
