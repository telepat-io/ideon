# Style: Journalistic

## Best Fit
Reported explainers, summary posts, event-driven context pieces.

## Rules

### Rule: Front-Load Verified Facts
Description: Lead with the most important confirmed information.
Negative example: Long preamble before key fact.
Positive example: First paragraph states event, impact, and timeline.

### Rule: Distinguish Reporting From Commentary
Description: Keep observed facts separate from interpretation.
Negative example: Blending personal opinion into factual summary lines.
Positive example: Reported facts first, analysis clearly labeled.

### Rule: Attribute Precisely
Description: Name sources or evidence boundaries explicitly.
Negative example: "Experts say..."
Positive example: "According to the Q1 operations report..."
