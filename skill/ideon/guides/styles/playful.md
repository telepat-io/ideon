# Style: Playful

## Best Fit
Creative explainers, lightweight brand voice, community content.

## Rules

### Rule: Keep the Joke Subordinate to the Point
Description: Humor should support comprehension, not replace it.
Negative example: Paragraph of jokes with no clear takeaway.
Positive example: One light line that makes the core point more memorable.

### Rule: Use Surprise With Restraint
Description: Unexpected phrasing should still preserve clarity.
Negative example: Metaphor-heavy intro that hides subject and outcome.
Positive example: One surprising comparison followed by direct explanation.

### Rule: Protect Reader Trust
Description: Do not exaggerate claims for comedic effect when facts matter.
Negative example: "This method literally fixes every writing problem ever."
Positive example: "This method is a useful default when your opening feels vague."
